CONSTANT VENTURE LLC

Helping companies to scale

Constant Venture LLC is a full-service IT consulting and custom application development product team focused on scaling startups and providing innovative technology solutions.

About

Constant Venture LLC was founded during the SXSW 2018 Hackathon where we created our first product, Legit Tix, a consumer fraud prevention application for event attendees. Legit Tix led to the Constant Venture pivot where we scale other similar solutions for clients across the globe.

accomplishments

Our journey when we signed up for the SXSW 2018 Hackathon in Austin, TX. Constant Venture developed Legit Tix, a consumer fraud prevention application for event attendees. Legit Tix allows consumers to buy 3rd party tickets from scalpers and to validate them before paying for the ticket insuring validity of the ticket.

A couple months later, Constant Venture was invited to the F8 Developer Conference Hackathon at Facebook in San Jose, California. CV developed Facebook Vibes, a solution that allows users with limited Wi-Fi to create connections through NDF and Bluetooth technologies. 

In June 2018, Constant Venture was invited to participate in the Capitol 360 Capitol Records Hackathon in Hollywood, California. CV won 2nd Place for best use of Microsoft Technologies by creating Hot Set, an automated setlist builder. This solution was for artists to engage with their fan base utilizing geolocation and live performance royalty redemption for the artist.

DevOps Engineer

Devops Engineers are IT professionals who collaborate with software developers, system operators and other IT staff members to manage code releases. They cross and merge the barriers that exist between software development, testing and operations teams and keep existing networks in mind as they design, plan and test.
