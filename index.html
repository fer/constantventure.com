Services

Home Management

  • Create and Post Listing
  • Manage availability
  • Air Filter Changing, Fire alarm testing, light bulbs, minor fixes etc.
  • Monthly report and invoices to owner
  • Dynamic pricing for rentals
  • Regular Communication with guests
  • Manage services such as cleaning, lawn care, window washing, pressure washing, etc.

PM Services

  • Manage all maintenance fixes required to keep the property top knotch
  • Coordinate and manage any household enhancements for the property, i.e. new dock, updating interior or any tasks requiring a licensed professional

Linen Services

  • As an added service CLS provides linen and towel turnover for vacation rentals.
  • CLS partners with linen facilities to provide cost effective and efficient turnover services maximizing on sameday guest change over.
  • CLS has the option to provide all or some of the linen inventory needed.

commitment

Homeowners rely on CLS to provide all the upkeep for their second homes, providing reassurance for their long term vacation home investment. CLS provides “boots on the ground” management providing cost effective solutions to protect and enhance the investment and return on your property. The value CLS provides include full service property management and management of monthly reports to homeowners on the status of their property. 

By incorporating CLS, tenant management is fully taken care of from booking to check out. CLS will also engage with other service providers in the area to address regular maintenance, turnover cleaning services and linen services as needed.
